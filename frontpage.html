About Me

Hello, my name is Kenny Robinson. I am a full time table games dealer at Jack Casino Cleveland. In my free time I play all sorts of games. I enjoy video games, board games, and especially tabletop rpgs like Dungeons and Dragons. I also enjoy designing my own board games and rpgs. I started a company for my game designs called Fuzzychan Games but I mostly design them for fun and show them to publishers with the vauge hope they will select them.

Additionally, I've started taking a web design bootcamp at Case Western Reserver University. I love my job at the casino and it has offered me some great opportunities. But I have decided after 5 years there that I want to move on. The bootcamp is offering me a great chance to learn a new skill and start a career in a more creative field.
